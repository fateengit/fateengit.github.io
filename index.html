My Musical Journey

I began learning piano at the age of 3 and my quest for learning western classical piano continues to grow immensely under the tutelage of Belarusian Concert pianists & Concert masters Natallia Kapylova and Liudmilla Alizarchyk who graduated from Belarus State Conservatory with musical lineage to J.S.Bach under Prof. Yushkevitch.

  • LCM Diploma with Distinction - one of the youngest Indians to accomplish the feat at the age of 12 yrs in December 2020 
  • Favourite Composers - Frédéric Chopin, Edvard Grieg, Johann Sebastian Bach, Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart, Ludwig Van Beethoven, Sergei Rachmaninov, Sergei Prokofiev, Claude Debussy, Franz Schubert, Felix Mendelssohn, Dmitri Shostakovich
  • Favourite Pianists - Martha Argerich, Evgeny Kissin, Daniil Trifonov, Grigory Sokolov, Alexander Malofeev, Seong Jin Cho, Ivo Pogorelic, Arthur Rubinstein, Vladimir Horowitz
